      About World on a Plate
      Our story

      🌍 World on a Plate is a passion project celebrating the incredible diversity of food across our planet. From the spicy street food of Bangkok to the refined bistros of Paris, every dish tells a story.

      Our mission is simple: make the world's best recipes accessible, inspiring, and fun to explore — no matter where you are.

      Built with ❤️ by food lovers, for food lovers.
